# 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of sales, products, suppliers, and pricing using Power BI. The goal is to uncover meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making in business operations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales volume across different product categories
* Understand supplier contribution and dependency
* Examine pricing distribution and variation
* Explore geographical distribution of suppliers
* Identify trends, patterns, and potential business opportunities

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following key components:

* **Products**: Product ID, Name, Category, Price
* **Suppliers**: Supplier ID, Name, Location
* **Orders**: Order details including Quantity and Sales
* **Categories**: Product classification

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 1. Sales Performance

* Sales volume varies significantly across product categories
* A few categories contribute the majority of total sales
* Some categories show low demand and require attention

---

### 2. Supplier Analysis

* Supplier contribution is uneven
* A small number of suppliers provide most products
* Indicates potential dependency on key suppliers

---

### 3. Pricing Distribution

* Product prices are right-skewed
* Majority of products fall in the low to mid price range
* Presence of high-priced outliers (premium products)

---

### 4. Pricing Variation Across Suppliers

* Suppliers follow different pricing strategies
* Some focus on budget products, while others offer premium items
* Significant variation in pricing across suppliers

---

### 5. Geographical Insights

* Suppliers are concentrated in specific regions
* Certain areas have higher supplier density
* Opportunities exist for expansion into underrepresented regions

---

## 📈 Visualizations Used

* Stacked Bar Chart → Sales by Category
* Treemap → Category contribution
* Bar Chart → Products per Supplier
* Box Plot → Price distribution & outliers
* Map Chart → Supplier locations
* Scatter Plot → Price vs Sales relationship

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

The analysis highlights that business performance is driven by a few key categories and suppliers. Pricing strategies vary significantly, and supplier distribution is geographically concentrated. These insights can help improve decision-making, optimize supply chains, and identify growth opportunities.
